Amazing story of Our Red Hat

Little Red Riding Hood, a kind girl, visits her grandma with treats. She meets a cunning wolf who tricks her and eats grandma. Red arrives, noticing the wolf, and cleverly saves grandma with a hunter’s help. They scare the wolf away. The story teaches about caution with strangers and bravery in helping others.
